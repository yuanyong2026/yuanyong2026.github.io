👨‍⚕️ About Dr. Yuan

我来自中国重庆，从事传统草本植物健康调理工作已超过40年。

我出生于1963年。那个年代生活条件艰苦，我从小体弱多病。3岁时患上幼年类风湿关节炎，父母带着我四处求医，但病情始终未能得到控制，最终导致四肢关节变形，落下了终身残疾。

正是这条漫长的求医之路，让我在13岁那年遇见了改变我一生的恩师。经过他的调理，我的病情终于得到了控制，不再继续恶化。18岁那年，我正式拜师学艺。

我自己做过病人，深知病痛对人的折磨。所以学得特别用心——那时心里只有一个念头：学成了，既能帮自己，也能帮别人。

我的师父是家传第四代中医。他的舅舅是新中国成立初期成都中医学院（现成都中医药大学）建院时的第一批教授。我跟着师父在诊所里学了整整6年。1981年，我通过了当地卫生局的考核，拿到了人生第一本行医执照，那年我24岁，正式成为一名中医执业者。之后我又在师父身边多待了几年，想在独立行医之前再多积累些经验。

1990年，我回到父母所在的城市，经当地卫生部门考核后取得了行医执照，一干就是几十年。1993年和1996年，我先后考取了"中医士"和"中医师"职称。后来我来到北京，在多家药房坐诊十余年。

四十多年来，我在临床实践和中医理论两个方面反复打磨——看的病人越多，越觉得学无止境。这些年来，经我调理后恢复健康的人已不计其数。

如今，我主要提供远程健康咨询服务：通过问诊了解您的身体状况，给出个性化的食疗级草本植物调理方案，帮助身体恢复平衡、重获健康。

💡 我想对您说：
我这一生，从病人变成医者。我太清楚一个人被病痛困住时的那种无助和焦虑。正是因为我经历过，所以我从不对病人说"你这个病没法治"——四十年来，我看到太多曾经被放弃的人，通过正确的调理找回了健康。如果您正在经历类似的困境，我希望我的经验可以帮到您。

I am from Chongqing, China, and I have spent over 40 years working with traditional plant-based healing methods to help people restore their health.

My journey into medicine did not begin as a practitioner, but as a patient. Born in 1963, I developed severe Juvenile Rheumatoid Arthritis at the age of 3. My parents took me from doctor to doctor, but nothing worked. By my teenage years, the disease had left me with permanent deformities in my limbs and a lifetime of physical challenges.

Then, at 13, I met the man who would change my life — my teacher, my mentor, the person I simply call "Master." He was a fourth-generation practitioner of Chinese herbal medicine, from a lineage that included one of the founding professors of Chengdu University of Traditional Chinese Medicine. Under his care, my condition finally stabilized. For the first time in years, I stopped getting worse.

At 18, I formally became his apprentice. That decision shaped everything that followed. I studied with him for six years in his clinic — watching, learning, and assisting. Here is the thing about being an apprentice: you do not just learn techniques; you absorb an entire way of thinking about health, one patient at a time. In 1981, at the age of 24, I passed the local health bureau examination and officially became a licensed TCM practitioner. Even then, I stayed on with my master for several more years — because when you see how much there is to learn, you do not rush to leave.

Over the decades that followed, I ran my own practice in my hometown, later moved to Beijing where I consulted at several clinics for over 10 years, and earned advanced certifications in 1993 and 1996. But credentials tell only a small part of the story. The real education happened face-to-face with patients — over 40 years, thousands of them — each one teaching me something new about how the human body heals.

Today, I offer remote health consultations. Through a detailed intake process and tongue analysis, I develop personalized protocols using dietary and traditional plant-based herbs — all aimed at helping your body regain its natural balance.

💡 A note from me to you:
I have lived on both sides of illness — as a patient who was told there was nothing more to be done, and as a practitioner who has watched people reclaim their health against the odds. Over 40 years, I have learned one thing above all: the body wants to heal. Sometimes it just needs the right support. If you are struggling with a health issue that has not responded to conventional approaches, I would be honored to see if my experience can help.
