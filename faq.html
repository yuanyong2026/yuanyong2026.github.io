📦 中药怎么寄到海外？

📦 How are the herbs shipped internationally?

药房代煎后真空包装，通过 DHL / FedEx 国际快递发出。通常 7-10 个工作日送达美国、加拿大、欧洲、澳洲等主要国家。真空包装的中药液海关通过率高。部分地区也可选择颗粒剂（免煎中药），体积更小。

Herbs are decocted by a pharmacy, vacuum-sealed, and shipped via DHL/FedEx — typically arriving within 7-10 business days to US, Canada, Europe, Australia, and most countries. Granule (instant) herbs are also available for certain regions.

⏱️ 什么时候能看到效果？

⏱️ How soon will I see results?

因病而异。急性问题如感冒咳嗽，1-3天即可见效；慢性病如湿疹、胃炎、月经不调，通常2-4周有明显改善；复杂慢性病如糖尿病、脂肪肝、肾功能问题，需1-3个月持续调理。袁大夫的病案记录了每次复诊的变化时间线，您可以参考类似病案的恢复速度。

It varies. Acute issues (cold, cough) may improve in 1-3 days; chronic conditions (eczema, gastritis, irregular menstruation) typically show improvement within 2-4 weeks; complex chronic issues (diabetes, fatty liver, kidney function) may require 1-3 months. Dr. Yuan's case records document the timeline of improvement for each condition — browse similar cases for reference.
